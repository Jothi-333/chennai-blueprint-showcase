**Design Movement:** Bauhaus Modernism meets Indian Contemporary

**Core Principles:**
- Geometric precision reflecting architectural blueprints
- Bold, asymmetric grid layouts inspired by floor plan diagrams
- Clean lines with strategic use of construction-inspired textures
- Functional beauty—every element serves the blueprint narrative

**Color Philosophy:**
Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) as the primary background, evoking blueprint paper in reverse. Accent with vibrant saffron (#FF6B35) for CTAs and highlights, representing Chennai's cultural warmth. Technical blue (#2E86AB) for diagrams and data points. Cream (#F7F7F2) for text, mimicking aged architectural drawings.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Diagonal-cut hero sections with overlapping floor plan wireframes. Content flows in L-shaped blocks mirroring the 2BHK layout itself. Sticky sidebar navigation that transforms into a miniature floor plan indicator as users scroll.

**Signature Elements:**
- Blueprint grid overlay (subtle dotted lines) across backgrounds
- Animated measurement callouts that appear on hover
- Isometric 3D building preview that rotates on scroll
- Construction tape-style dividers between sections

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Interactions mimic architectural drafting—hover states reveal dimension annotations, clicks "stamp" approval marks, scroll triggers blueprint roll-out animations. Smooth, deliberate transitions that feel like flipping through construction documents.

**Animation:**
Entrance animations simulate blueprint unfurling from rolled paper. Section transitions use diagonal wipes mimicking page turns. Floor plan diagrams draw themselves with SVG line animations. Subtle parallax on building elevation views.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Space Grotesk" (700) for headings—geometric, architectural
- Body: "Inter" (400, 500) for specifications and descriptions
- Technical: "JetBrains Mono" (500) for measurements and data
Hierarchy enforced through size contrast (48px/24px/16px) and strategic weight shifts.

**Design Movement:** Swiss Minimalism with Tropical Modernism

**Core Principles:**
- Extreme whitespace as structural element
- Monochromatic palette with single accent color
- Grid-based precision with intentional breaks
- Content breathes—never cramped or cluttered

**Color Philosophy:**
Pure white (#FFFFFF) base with soft grey (#F5F5F5) for cards. Single accent: terracotta red (#C1440E) inspired by Chennai's clay roofs. All diagrams rendered in grayscale with red highlights for key features. Text in deep charcoal (#2D2D2D) for maximum readability.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Strict 12-column grid with generous gutters. Hero section uses full-bleed imagery with minimal text overlay. Content organized in card-based modules that float on white space. Asymmetric placement—never centered unless intentional.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin red border accents on hover states
- Oversized floor plan thumbnails with zoom-on-click
- Numerical data presented in large, bold typography
- Minimal iconography—only essential symbols

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Restrained and purposeful. Hover states add subtle shadows, not color changes. Clicks feel decisive with quick fade transitions. Scrolling reveals content through gentle fade-ups, never slides or bounces.

**Animation:**
Micro-interactions only—button presses, card lifts on hover, smooth page transitions. Floor plans fade in sequentially (ground → first → second → terrace). No decorative animations; every motion serves clarity.

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Manrope" (800) for section titles—clean, modern
- Body: "IBM Plex Sans" (400, 600) for all text
- Data: "IBM Plex Mono" (500) for specs
Strict size scale: 64px/32px/18px/14px. Line height 1.6 for body, 1.2 for headings.

**Core Principles:**
- Raw, unpolished edges with intentional imperfection
- Bold, clashing colors that demand attention
- Thick borders and heavy shadows for depth
- Playful chaos within structured containers

**Color Philosophy:**
Vibrant mustard yellow (#F4A300) as primary background. Punchy magenta (#E91E63) for interactive elements. Deep teal (#006D77) for informational sections. Black (#000000) for text and borders. Each floor plan gets its own color zone—ground (grey), first (coral), second (mint), terrace (sky blue).

**Layout Paradigm:**
Broken grid—sections intentionally misaligned with visible borders. Hero section uses diagonal split with contrasting colors. Content blocks have thick (8px) black borders with offset shadows. Floor plans displayed in tilted frames like pinned sketches.

**Signature Elements:**
- Chunky, hand-drawn-style arrows pointing to features
- Stamp-like badges ("2BHK", "North Facing", "Lift Included")
- Rough-edged image masks for photos
- Overlapping layers with visible z-index stacking

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Loud and immediate. Buttons grow and shift shadows on hover. Clicks trigger bold color flashes. Scroll reveals content with bouncy spring animations. Every interaction feels tactile and physical.

Exaggerated entrance animations—elements slide in from off-screen with overshoot. Floor plans "drop" into place with bounce. Hover states trigger playful rotations and scale changes. Parallax effects on background elements.

- Display: "Archivo Black" (900) for headlines—bold, unapologetic
- Body: "Work Sans" (500, 700) for readability with character

## Selected Design: Bauhaus Modernism meets Indian Contemporary

This approach perfectly balances professional architectural presentation with cultural warmth. The geometric precision honors the blueprint's technical nature while the saffron accents and diagonal layouts add visual interest. The blueprint grid overlay and animated measurement callouts will make stakeholders feel they're exploring a living architectural document.
